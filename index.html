Submarine Two

Airtight Mutitasking Browser


⎋ Terms and Conditions

More than a Reddit app. A desktop-quality multitasking experience brought to your fingertips. Tap and hold on anything to open it in the background. Get around Submarine Two using the Dock. Drag and drop to rearrange and set your favorite thing as your homepage! Other main features include:

  • Swipe to vote, save, interact with everything (customizable)
  • iOS 13, Dark Mode, Homescreen Shortcuts, Dynamic Type support
  • Multireddit, Moderator, Wiki, and more advanced Reddit feature support
  • Fast, reliable navigation with inline searching to find things easily
  • Minimal and pragmatic design with compact or large thumbnail mode
